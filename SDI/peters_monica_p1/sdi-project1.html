Akwesasne Reservation Adventure

I live on a tiny island, in the middle of the St. Lawrence River, in a Mohawk Indian Reservation. Our reservation has the borders of USA and Canada running through the middle of some of our homes and buildings.

Each time any Mohawk leaves or returns from our reservation, they are usually stopped, questioned, and searched at a minimum of 2 or more 'checkpoints' by border guards, police, federal agents, and various military from the USA and Canada.

November 24th 2011, Monica Peters
